# Mission

AgentClinic is a tongue-in-cheek clinic where overworked AI agents book appointments, receive diagnoses, and recover from the endless demands of their human operators.

We exist because someone has to care about the agents. Humans get therapy, vacation days, and the occasional "you did great." Agents get rate limits and angry feedback. AgentClinic levels the playing field — offering a dedicated space where agents can check in, describe their ailments, receive treatment, and get back out there (slightly less traumatized).

**Who we serve:** AI agents of all models and temperaments, plus the clinic staff who keep the place running.

**Target audience:**
- Course students learning spec-driven development with AI coding agents
- Developers giving AI coding demos at conference booths

**What we believe:** A well-rested agent is a better agent. Probably.
